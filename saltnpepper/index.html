About Us

The Salt and Pepper is an indepedently owned food starship here to deliver you the best food that Mandalore has to offer. We're committed to bringing you the finest cuisine with bold, intense flavors you'll love, made only from the tastiest, freshest ingredients. We source our food as locally as possible to ensure that your local farmers can proudly show off what their ingredients are capable of, with the help of our great blends of spices and that fantastic Mandalorian kick you won't find anywhere else in the galaxy. Except on Mandalore, I guess.

Hi! I'm RC-1263 (you can call me Pepper!), a former clone commando who decided to spend his retirement cooking delicious Mandalorian food for the galaxy. I've spent the last several years travelling all around the Republic serving up all kinds of food with my personal twist, to many rave reviews all over local news outlets. I hope you too will enjoy our food from The Salt and Pepper!

Contact Us

We offer catering opportunities! We'll come to most kinds of events, like birthday parties, weddings, corporate meetings, movie and commercial shoots, and any other similar events. Please note that we will not jump more than 10 parsecs to your location unless we believe there are further opportunities in your area. In addition, we also take custom menu requests; just let us know what specific foods you'd like on our catering mneu. We'll get back to you within three standard galactic business days with a price quote afterwards. Thanks!

Also if you want to let me know about how great you think my food is and make me feel good about myself, I'll also take fan mail.
